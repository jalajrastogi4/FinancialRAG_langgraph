## How did Amazon's core business segments, such as AWS, Online Stores, and Advertising, perform individually and contribute to overall financial results in 2023 and 2024?

### Focused Query 1: Amazon AWS revenue and operating income 2023 2024

**Key Findings:**
Amazon Web Services (AWS) demonstrated significant growth in both net sales and operating income during 2023 and 2024.

*   **Net Sales:**
    *   **2023:** $90,757 million
    *   **2024:** $107,556 million
    *   This represents a year-over-year growth of approximately 18.5% from 2023 to 2024.
*   **Operating Income:**
    *   **2023:** $24,631 million
    *   **2024:** $39,834 million
    *   This shows a substantial increase of approximately 61.7% from 2023 to 2024, indicating improved profitability and operational efficiency within AWS.
*   **Contribution to Overall Results:** AWS is a major contributor to Amazon's overall operating income, consistently being the most profitable segment. For example, in 2024, AWS operating income of $39,834 million represented a significant portion of the consolidated operating income of $68,593 million.

### Focused Query 2: Amazon Online Stores revenue and operating income 2023 2024

**Key Findings:**
Amazon's Online Stores segment continued to generate substantial revenue, although its operating income is reported within broader geographic segments.

*   **Net Sales (Online Stores):**
    *   **2023:** $231,872 million
    *   **2024:** $247,029 million
    *   This indicates a growth of approximately 6.5% from 2023 to 2024.
*   **Operating Income (North America and International segments which include Online Stores):**
    *   **North America Segment Operating Income:**
        *   **2023:** $14,877 million
        *   **2024:** $24,967 million
    *   **International Segment Operating Income (Loss):**
        *   **2023:** $(2,656) million
        *   **2024:** $3,792 million
    *   While specific operating income for "Online Stores" is not separately disclosed, the North America segment, which includes online and physical stores, advertising, and subscription services, showed a significant improvement in operating income. The International segment also moved from an operating loss in 2023 to an operating income in 2024, contributing positively to overall results.

### Focused Query 3: Amazon Advertising revenue and operating income 2023 2024

**Key Findings:**
Amazon's Advertising services demonstrated strong revenue growth in 2023 and 2024, contributing to overall sales. Similar to Online Stores, its operating income is integrated into broader segments.

*   **Net Sales (Advertising Services):**
    *   **2023:** $46,906 million
    *   **2024:** $56,214 million
    *   This represents a growth of approximately 19.8% from 2023 to 2024.
*   **Operating Income (North America and International segments which include Advertising Services):**
    *   As noted above, the operating income for Advertising Services is not reported as a standalone segment. Its profitability contributes to the overall operating income of the North America and International segments, which showed positive trends in 2024.

### Focused Query 4: Amazon overall financial results 2023 2024

**Key Findings:**
Amazon's consolidated financial results showed strong growth in net sales, operating income, and net income in 2023 and 2024.

*   **Consolidated Net Sales:**
    *   **2023:** $574,785 million
    *   **2024:** $637,959 million
    *   This represents a growth of approximately 11.0% from 2023 to 2024.
*   **Consolidated Operating Income:**
    *   **2023:** $36,852 million
    *   **2024:** $68,593 million
    *   This is a significant increase of approximately 86.1% from 2023 to 2024, indicating improved overall profitability.
*   **Consolidated Net Income:**
    *   **2023:** $30,425 million
    *   **2024:** $59,248 million
    *   The net income almost doubled from 2023 to 2024, highlighting a very strong financial performance.

### Summary
Amazon's core business segments, AWS, Online Stores, and Advertising, all demonstrated positive performance in 2023 and 2024, contributing significantly to the company's strong overall financial results.

AWS was the standout performer in terms of profitability, with substantial growth in both net sales and operating income. Its operating income in 2024 was a major driver of Amazon's consolidated operating income.

Online Stores and Advertising services also saw healthy increases in net sales. While their specific operating income is not individually segmented, their performance contributes to the improved profitability seen in the broader North America and International segments. The North America segment transitioned from an operating loss in 2022 to significant operating income in 2023 and 2024, and the International segment moved from a loss in 2023 to an operating profit in 2024.

Overall, Amazon experienced robust financial growth from 2023 to 2024, with consolidated net sales increasing by 11.0%, operating income by 86.1%, and net income nearly doubling. This indicates successful strategies across its diverse business segments, with AWS continuing to be a highly profitable engine and the retail and advertising arms showing strong revenue generation and improved profitability within their respective broader segments.
