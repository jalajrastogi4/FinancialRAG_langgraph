## 2. How did Amazon's profitability metrics, including net income, operating income, and margins, evolve in 2023 and 2024?

[cite_start]Amazon achieved a significant profitability turnaround in 2023. Operating income for the full year 2023 reached **$36,852 million**, a substantial increase from $12,248 million in 2022[cite: 3].

### Key Profitability Shifts
* [cite_start]**Net Income Rebound**: Amazon reported a net income of $30,425 million in 2023, recovering from a net loss of $(2,722) million in 2022[cite: 3].
* [cite_start]**North America Profitability**: The segment returned to profitability with $14,877 million in operating income in 2023, compared to an operating loss of $(2,847) million the previous year[cite: 4].
* [cite_start]**International Improvement**: The International operating loss narrowed significantly to $(2,656) million from $(7,746) million in 2022[cite: 4].
* [cite_start]**AWS Contributions**: AWS remained the primary profit driver, contributing $24,631 million in operating income in 2023[cite: 4].

[cite_start]For 2024, Amazon projected continued margin improvement, with Q1 operating income expected to be between **$8.0 billion and $12.0 billion**, compared to $4.8 billion in Q1 2023[cite: 2]. [cite_start]This guidance included a $0.9 billion benefit from lower depreciation expense due to changes in server useful life[cite: 2].

## 4. What were Amazon's cash flow from operations, capital expenditures, and free cash flow trends in 2023 and 2024?

Amazon demonstrated a strong positive trend in cash flow metrics across 2023 and 2024.

### Operating Cash Flow
* **Full Year 2023**: Net cash provided by operating activities was $84,946 million.
* **Full Year 2024**: Increased to $115,877 million.
* **Quarterly Trends**: Trailing twelve-month (TTM) figures showed consistent growth, rising from $54,330 million in March 2023 to $112,706 million by September 2024.

### Capital Expenditures (CapEx)
* **Full Year 2023**: Net capital expenditures (purchases of property and equipment, net of proceeds) were $(48,133) million.
* **Full Year 2024**: Net capital expenditures increased significantly to $(77,658) million.
* **TTM Insight**: While TTM CapEx saw a temporary decrease in March 2024 ($48,998 million), it rose to $(69,753) million by September 2024.

### Free Cash Flow (FCF)
FCF experienced a remarkable turnaround:
* **March 31, 2023 (TTM)**: Negative $(3,319) million.
* **March 31, 2024 (TTM)**: Surged to $50,149 million.
* **September 30, 2024 (TTM)**: Stood at $42,953 million, reflecting increased capital investment.
