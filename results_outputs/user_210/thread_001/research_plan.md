# Research Plan

## User Query
Do a detailed analysis of Amazon's financial performance in 2023 and 2024

## Thematic Questions

1. What was Amazon's overall revenue and growth trajectory in 2023 and the reported periods of 2024 across its main segments?
2. How did Amazon's profitability metrics, including net income, operating income, and margins, evolve in 2023 and 2024?
3. What were the key drivers and challenges impacting the performance of Amazon's core business segments (e.g., AWS, Online Stores, Advertising) during 2023 and 2024?
4. What were Amazon's cash flow from operations, capital expenditures, and free cash flow trends in 2023 and 2024?
5. What was Amazon's financial outlook and guidance provided for 2024, considering the performance and market conditions of 2023?
