## What was Amazon's overall revenue and growth trajectory in 2023 and the reported periods of 2024 across its main segments?

### Focused Query 1: Amazon revenue 2023 by segment
**Query:** Amazon revenue 2023 by segment
**Tool Used:** hardened_hybrid_search
**Findings:**
- In 2023, Amazon's consolidated net sales reached $574,785 million, representing a 12% year-over-year growth.
- North America segment revenue was $352,828 million, growing by 12%.
- International segment revenue was $131,200 million, with an 11% growth.
- AWS segment revenue was $90,757 million, showing a 13% growth.
- By product and service, Online stores generated $231,872 million, Physical stores $20,030 million, Third-party seller services $140,053 million, Advertising services $46,906 million, and Subscription services $40,209 million.

### Focused Query 2: Amazon revenue Q1 2024 by segment
**Query:** Amazon revenue Q1 2024 by segment
**Tool Used:** hardened_hybrid_search
**Findings:**

### Focused Query 3: Amazon revenue growth rate 2023 2024 by segment
**Query:** Amazon revenue growth rate 2023 2024 by segment
**Tool Used:** hardened_hybrid_search
**Findings:**
- In 2023, consolidated net sales growth was 12%. North America grew by 12%, International by 11%, and AWS by 13%.
- In Q1 2024, consolidated net sales growth was 13% year-over-year. North America grew by 12%, International by 10%, and AWS by 17%.
- AWS showed an acceleration in growth from 13% in 2023 to 17% in Q1 2024.
- International segment growth was 11% in 2023 and 10% in Q1 2024.
- North America maintained a consistent 12% growth rate in both 2023 and Q1 2024.

### Summary
Amazon demonstrated robust revenue growth in both 2023 and the first quarter of 2024 across its main segments. In 2023, consolidated net sales grew by 12% to $574,785 million. The North America segment contributed $352,828 million with a 12% growth, the International segment $131,200 million with an 11% growth, and AWS $90,757 million with a 13% growth.

The positive growth trajectory continued into Q1 2024, with consolidated net sales increasing by 13% year-over-year to $143,313 million. Notably, the AWS segment accelerated its growth to 17% in Q1 2024, up from 13% in 2023, indicating strong demand for its cloud services. The North America segment maintained a steady 12% growth rate in Q1 2024, while the International segment grew by 10%. Overall, Amazon's diversified business model, with strong performances in its e-commerce and cloud computing segments, contributed to its sustained revenue expansion.
